This small project display the simulation of the growth of cucumber blossoms.

Based on the experimental data from this paper, we can speculate that future climate conditions such as high temperature, high precipitation, and acid rain may alter the nitrogen/phosphorus ratio in soil, thereby influencing the floral traits of cucumbers.

I built the cucumber vine using Blender’s Geometry Nodes, adjusting the parameters to create an exaggerated flowering effect.

During the cucumber’s blooming process, the sky color and sun size gradually change, suggesting a rising temperature in the environment.

Meanwhile, the interplay between the flat, halftone sky, the halftone, comic-inspired sun, and the realistically rendered cucumber vine creates a striking contrast that further amplifies the sense of exaggeration and artificiality.
